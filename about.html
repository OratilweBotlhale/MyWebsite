....I’m pretty much just a pretty geek.

Hi, I'm Oratilwe but since we’re friends now, call me Aura!


I’m literally a 19-year-old, trying to make it by in life while doing what I Iove. I was born and raised in Johannesburg South, South Africa but currently based in the Vaal.


I'm a creative at heart with a love for fashion, makeup, and digital storytelling. For me, getting dressed, applying makeup, or planning a shoot isn’t just about looking good — it’s about expressing who I am and what I stand for. Content creation allows me to turn everyday moments into visual art, connect with people who share my energy, and inspire confidence through creativity.


At the same time, I’m deeply passionate about technology. As a 3rd-year IT student, I spend just as much time behind the screen as I do in front of the camera. I believe in breaking stereotypes — proving that you can code and contour, debug and design, all in the same day. I’m building a path where beauty and tech collide, and I want to show other young women that they don’t have to choose between their passions. They can own all of them.
